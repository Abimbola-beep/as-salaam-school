About AS-SALAAM Community Group of Schools

AS-SALAAM Community Group of Schools is a co-educational institution located in Ibadan offering Creche, Nursery, Primary and College education.

We focus on academic excellence, good moral conduct, Quranic memorization and a serene academic environment supported by skilled, qualified and God-fearing teachers.
